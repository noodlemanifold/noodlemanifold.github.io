Rowland's Adventure

This game was my entry to the Mini Jam 107 game jam on Itch.io, where the theme was "Gravity is Under Your Control". I made a cute little 3D platformer, with the twist that when you jump, all other physics objects jump as well, almost like when you jump you flip gravity! I had lots of ambition for this project, but it was way too much for a 3 day jam, and in the end I barely managed to finish a much smaller version. It is still lots of fun and I'm proud of what I could make in such a short time!
